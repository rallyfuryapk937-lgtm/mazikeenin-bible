Does the Bible Ever Mention Mazikeen?

The answer is simple: No.

The name Mazikeen in the Bible cannot be found anywhere in the Old Testament or the New Testament. Although the Bible speaks about angels, demons, and spiritual warfare, it never identifies a figure named Mazikeen.

Why Is There So Much Confusion?

  • Popular TV shows use the character Mazikeen.
  • Comic books created fictional stories around the name.
  • Social media often mixes folklore with biblical facts.
  • Many readers assume every supernatural character comes from the Bible.

The Historical Background

The name is associated with the Hebrew word Mazikin, which appears in Jewish folklore rather than the Bible. These writings describe harmful spirits, but they are not part of biblical Scripture.
